Project Details

     

    1/2014

    Construction, Image
    Date : January 2014
    Client : Private
    Categories : Construction

    The first 2014 guitar numbered "1/2014" features Western Australian Sheok (Allocasuarina fraseriana) sides and back with a Bunya Pine (Araucaria bidwillii) top and Tasmanian Oak (Eucalyptus delegatensis / Eucalyptus regnans* / Eucalyptus obliqua) neck. The fret board is Gidgee (Acacia cambadgei) and the bridge is Cooba (Acacia salicina). The bindings are also Tasmanian Oak whilst the back bracing and linings are Queensland Maple (Flindersia brayleyana).
